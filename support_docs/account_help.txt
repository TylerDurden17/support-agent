Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email link to reset it. The link expires in 15 minutes.

Q: I want to delete my account permanently.
A: To permanently delete your account, go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. This action cannot be undone.

Q: How do I change my profile photo?
A: Navigate to your profile, click on the avatar, and upload a new image (JPEG/PNG under 5MB).

Q: I didn’t receive the email verification link.
A: Check your spam folder. If it’s not there, click "Resend verification" on the login page.

Q: Can I merge two accounts?
A: Account merging is not currently supported. You can manually transfer data by exporting and importing between accounts.
