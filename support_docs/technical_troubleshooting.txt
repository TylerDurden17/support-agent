Q: The app crashes when I upload a file. What should I do?
A: Try clearing the app cache and reinstalling. If the issue persists, send us a screen recording and your device details.

Q: I’m getting a 500 error when logging in.
A: This is likely a temporary server issue. Please try again in a few minutes. If it continues, report it with your browser and timestamp.

Q: I can’t download files from my dashboard.
A: Check your internet connection and disable any ad blockers. If the problem persists, try a different browser.

Q: The notification sound isn’t working.
A: Ensure sound is enabled in your system and app settings. Also, check your device’s "Do Not Disturb" mode.

Q: I get logged out automatically every few hours.
A: For security, we log users out after 12 hours of inactivity. Enable "Keep me signed in" if supported.
